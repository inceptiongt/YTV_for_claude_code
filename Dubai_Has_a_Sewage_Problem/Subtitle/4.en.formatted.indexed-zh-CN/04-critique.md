## Accuracy Issues

**[2]** "Mega projects check" → "超级工程——" — original is a checklist item ("mega projects ✓"), not a label. Should be "超级工程，✓" or "大型项目✓" to preserve the checklist humor.

**[7]** "Oh. Oh. Okay." → "哦。哦。好吧。" — the three exclamations each are a segment; the translation works but feels flat. Consider "哦。哦。行吧。" — more spoken.

**[8]** "blingiest city on Earth" → "最浮华的城市" — "土豪感最强的城市" is slightly more colloquial and closer to "blingy" (ostentatious/flashy wealth).

**[9]** "a plan is a foot" → "计划已经在推进了" — loses the "afoot" pun entirely. Should note the wordplay or adapt: "计划已经上马了" keeps the idiom spirit better. "in case you're thinking of swapping your Lamborghini for a dinghy" — this belongs in [38], not [9].

**[17]** "mahoosive icon" → "超级巨无霸地标" — slightly redundant ("超级" + "巨无霸"). "这座巨无霸地标" is cleaner.

**[18]** "more is more and less is snore" → "多即是好，少即是无聊" — the rhyme/rhythm is lost. Consider: "多即是美，少即乏味" — preserves the rhyme better.

**[20]** "That's not entirely correct," — this appears at end of [20] in English, but the draft moves into [21] naturally. Check: the comma at end of [20] should lead into [21]'s "但真相也好不到哪里去". ✓ OK.

**[22]** "cherry on top" → "压垮骆驼的最后一根稻草" — this inverts the meaning. "Cherry on top" means an added delight/bonus on something bad, i.e., making an already-bad situation worse in an ironic way. "The last straw" fits the meaning here (adding to overload). ✓ Actually the meaning does work in context — the Burj Khalifa was the "crowning irony" on top of an already-overloaded system. Both idioms convey "the final addition to something already excessive." Keep.

**[38]** "a plan is a foot" → "计划已经在推进了" — loses the wordplay. "已经有计划了" or "行动已经开始了" is fine. The original has a pun: "afoot" (underway) vs "a foot" (unit of measurement). Hard to replicate; current translation acceptable but flat.

**[40]** "keep it regular" → "保持'畅通'" — the quotes on 畅通 are good, it's a euphemism for regular bowel movements. ✓

**[45]** "the call of nature is going to be assisted by well, nature" → "大自然的召唤就将由……大自然来协助完成" — ✓ Good wordplay preserved.

**[47]** "cargo" → "货物" is implied but not stated in draft; draft says "所有污水将汇聚到..." — the word "cargo" (货物, used euphemistically for sewage) was dropped. The euphemism adds humor. Consider: "这批'货物'将被提升最后几米"

**[50]** "Construction is split into four different phases. The first contracts were signed with construction companies in 2025" — this is one line [50]; draft correctly keeps it as one line. ✓

**[54]** The financial jargon joke — draft is good, deliberately overcomplicated. ✓
