## Domain
Urban infrastructure / engineering documentary — YouTube video by The B1M channel about Dubai's sewage problem and the Dubai Strategic Sewage Tunnels project.

## Tone & Register
Conversational, witty, slightly irreverent. The host (Fred Mills / The B1M) uses British English idioms, humor, and self-deprecating jokes. The tone alternates between entertaining narrative and informative exposition. Not academic — think "enthusiastic documentary presenter."

- Direct address to audience: "guys", "you", rhetorical questions
- Filler speech: "uh", ">>", "[clears throat]"
