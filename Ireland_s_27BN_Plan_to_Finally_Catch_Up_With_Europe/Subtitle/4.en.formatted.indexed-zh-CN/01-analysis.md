# Content Analysis

## Domain & Genre
Infrastructure journalism / urban planning documentary narration. YouTube video transcript (The B1M channel) about Dublin MetroLink — Ireland's long-delayed first metro system.

## Tone & Style
- Conversational, engaging, occasionally humorous and self-deprecating
- Mix of narration (presenter's voice) and interview snippets (marked with `>>`)
- Ironic undertone when discussing decades of delays and broken promises
- Sponsor segment (lines [83]–[94], [178]–[180]) is more promotional/light-hearted

## Source Language Characteristics
- Colloquial English with Irish expressions ("the bloody thing", "wee bit", "faff around")
- Interview clips contain natural speech patterns, repetitions, fillers ("um uh", "and and", "building building")
- Line [4] "They're doing my" is incomplete/cut-off — preserve as-is
- `>>` marks speaker changes (interviewees)
