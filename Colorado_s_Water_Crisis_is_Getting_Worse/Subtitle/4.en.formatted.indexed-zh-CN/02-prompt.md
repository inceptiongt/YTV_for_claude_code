# Translation Prompt

## Task
Translate a YouTube documentary subtitle file from English to Simplified Chinese (zh-CN).

## Style
Conversational-documentary. Natural spoken Chinese — as if a Chinese narrator and interviewees are speaking. Engaging, accessible, not stiff.

## CRITICAL Format Rules
1. Each input line `[N] English text` → `[N] 中文文本` — ONE line per number, no exceptions
2. `>>` speaker markers: keep on the same line, e.g., `[7] >> 这真的是第一次……`
3. `# Chapter headings`: translate the heading text, keep `#` prefix
4. Do NOT add blank lines between numbered entries
5. Do NOT merge two [N] lines into one, do NOT split one [N] line into two
6. Filler words "uh", "um" → omit in Chinese
