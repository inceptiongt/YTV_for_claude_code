## Domain & Genre
Infrastructure/civil engineering documentary (YouTube). Mix of narration and on-camera interviews (marked with `>>`).

## Tone
Conversational-documentary: engaging, accessible to general audience. Occasional humor (e.g., the "Gross" name joke). Interviews carry a more formal/official tone.

## Format Constraints (CRITICAL)
- Numbered segments `[N]` — must be preserved exactly, one Chinese line per English line
- `>>` speaker markers must be preserved as-is
- `# Chapter headings` must be translated
- No merging or splitting of lines
