# Content Analysis

## Domain
YouTube video transcript about the history and urban development of Amsterdam, Netherlands. Covers geography, medieval city planning, the Dutch Golden Age, canal belt construction, and modern development.

## Tone
- Conversational, documentary-style narration
- Mix of host narration and expert interviews (marked with >>)
- Educational but engaging, aimed at general audience
- Includes a sponsored ad segment

## Key Terminology
- Amstel/Amstl/Amsel: River Amstel (阿姆斯特尔河)
- Dam Square: 水坝广场
- Pete: peat (泥炭)
- Dyke/zyke: dike (堤坝)
- Canal belt: 运河带
- Dutch Golden Age: 荷兰黄金时代
- Dutch East India Company: 荷兰东印度公司
- Jordan/Yordan area: 约旦区
- Plantagia: plantation area (种植园区)
- Bastion ring: 棱堡环
- Reformation: 宗教改革
- Alterasi: Alteratie (阿姆斯特丹改宗事件)

## Translation Challenges
1. Must preserve [N] index prefix for every line
2. Must preserve >> speaker markers
3. Must preserve # chapter headings
4. Lines must stay 1:1 - no merging or splitting
5. Some lines have transcription errors or odd phrasing that need interpretation
6. Historical terms and Dutch names need consistent translation
7. URL/promo text should be preserved as-is

## Style
Conversational, natural Chinese that reads like a documentary narration. Preserve the host's enthusiasm and the experts' authoritative but accessible tone.
