# Translation Prompt

## Task
Translate an English video transcript (documentary style) into Chinese (zh-CN).

## Style & Audience
- Style: **conversational** — casual, approachable, as if narrating to a friend
- Audience: general Chinese readers
- The source is a video narration transcript, so keep it lively and flowing

## Critical Format Rules (MUST follow)
1. **Preserve every [N] prefix exactly** — every numbered line must start with `[N]` in the output
2. **One-to-one line correspondence** — [1] → [1], [2] → [2], … [142] → [142]. Do NOT merge or split lines
3. **Translate all `# chapter heading` lines** into Chinese
4. **Keep `>>` speaker quote markers** at the start of lines that have them
5. **Keep `[snorts]`** exactly as-is in line [134]
6. **Keep brand names** ODU/Odoo as-is

## Translation Principles
- Rewrite into natural, engaging Chinese — not word-for-word
- For informal exclamations ("hey presto", "bring most of us out in a cold sweat"), use idiomatic Chinese equivalents
- Keep the enthusiastic, documentary narrator voice
- Speaker quotes (>>) should sound like spontaneous speech
- Keep engineering terms accurate
- Keep numbers, units, and data points exactly as in source
