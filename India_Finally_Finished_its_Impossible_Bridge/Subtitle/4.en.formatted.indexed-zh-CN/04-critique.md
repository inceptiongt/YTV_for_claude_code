# Critical Review

## Accuracy Issues

1. **[2] "Reesei"** — likely a mishearing/OCR error in the transcript; kept as-is since the original is unclear. No fix needed at translation level.

2. **[6] "Janab Bridge"** → 贾纳布大桥 — but in [23], [24], [26], [27], [32], etc. "Chenab/Chinab/Chennab" is used. In the original transcript these may refer to the same bridge. In the draft, "Janab" was rendered as 贾纳布大桥 in [6] and "切纳布大桥" elsewhere — **inconsistency**. Should standardize: keep 贾纳布大桥 for explicit "Janab Bridge" mentions (it may refer to the site) and 切纳布大桥 for "Chenab/Chinab/Chennab Bridge" mentions.

3. **[27] "169m"** — "m" likely means "mile" (169 mi all-weather rail line). Draft says "169公里" which is wrong. Should be "169英里" (or keep "169km" if the source intends metric — but "m" here is ambiguous). Given context (it's a very long rail line), 169 miles (~272 km) is more plausible. **Fix: 169英里**.

4. **[34] "allin reports"** — likely "all-in reports" meaning comprehensive/overall. Draft translates as "据各方报告称" — acceptable.

5. **[41] "cut the faf"** — "faf" is British slang for "fuss/faff". Draft says "帮你大幅减少繁琐的工作" — accurate in meaning.

6. **[61]** The original says "just to make this extraordinary location even remotely accessible." — This is a continuation sentence, fragment. Draft [61] renders as a fragment too — correct.

7. **[91] "stabilize this bridge with an incredibly strong arch"** — The speaker is talking about the importance of the arch's stability. Draft says "能用无比强韧的拱桥来稳固这座大桥至关重要" — slightly circular (stabilizing a bridge with a bridge). Should be: "用一个无比强劲的拱来稳固这座大桥至关重要".

8. **[109] Speaker quote logic** — Original: "If you have the span relatively that shaking is twice as much because the shaking is a fixed amount." — This is a confusing/unclear sentence in the original. Draft rendition captures the gist but is also somewhat unclear. Keep close to original intent.

## Europeanized Language Issues

1. **[4]** "你可以想象，试图在这里建造一座破纪录大桥的施工队面临着怎样的挑战" — somewhat stiff. More natural: "不难想象，试图在这里建造一座破纪录大桥的施工团队面临着多大的挑战。"

2. **[17]** "即便老天爷给你放一天假，也不能保证地震不会突然将大地剧烈震动" — Good idiomatic rendering.

3. **[31]** "反对者认为，它不过是加强对该地区控制的一种手段" — Good.

4. **[60]** "而答案，相当不可思议" — Good but can be stronger: "而这个答案，着实令人叹为观止。"

5. **[93]** "就像一座空中秋千，或者一条由缆索构成的单轨" — Original says "aerial trapze" (trapeze). "空中秋千" is loose but understandable. More accurate: "有点像高空走钢丝，或者由缆索构成的单轨道。"

6. **[97]** "大功告成，一座拱桥就这样诞生了" — Good idiomatic rendering for "hey presto, you've got an arch."

7. **[115]** Long sentence in draft, slightly awkward with the parenthetical. Restructure for clarity.
