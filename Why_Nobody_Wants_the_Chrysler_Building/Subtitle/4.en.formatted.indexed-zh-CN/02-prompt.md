# Translation Prompt

## Task
Translate a YouTube subtitle file (English → Simplified Chinese) for a documentary video about the Chrysler Building in New York City.

## Target Language
zh-CN (Simplified Chinese)

## Audience & Style
- General audience (非专业观众)
- Conversational, natural-flowing (口语化、自然流畅)
- NOT literal/Europeanized — express ideas as a Chinese speaker would naturally say them
- Match the energetic, engaging tone of the narrator

## Format Rules
1. Every [N] line MUST be preserved with its exact [N] prefix
2. Chapter headings (#) must be translated
3. Speaker-change marker >> must be preserved
4. Same number of [N] lines as source (216 total)
5. Do not merge or split [N] lines
